Data Scraping

I built a scraper in Python to scrape house price data from GOV.UK. After formatting the data I scraped, I designed a bar chart to represent house prices in different regions of the UK. I also made this chart interactive - try hovering over the bars.

Data Stories

Chris Giles of the Financial Times claimed that the economy had adapted well to COVID and lockdowns such that the reduction in GDP following the second UK lockdown was far smaller. Using ONS GDP data, I calculated the percentage loss to GDP following each lockdown and charted my results. This chart supports Chris’ statement – the reduction in GDP following the second lockdown was roughly 16 percentage points lower.
